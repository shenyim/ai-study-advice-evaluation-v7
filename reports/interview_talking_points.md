# Interview Talking Points

- The project evaluates AI advice as a socio-technical system, not just as text generation.
- The core metric is risk-weighted quality because high-impact advice should be held to a stricter standard.
- The review queue is the product bridge: it turns model failures into backlog items with owners and fixes.
- The human annotation workflow includes rater coverage and reliability because human scores are not automatically objective.
- The statistical layer uses bootstrap confidence intervals to avoid overclaiming from small samples.
- The honest limitation is that the current demo is not a deployed student-advice product or a large commercial LLM benchmark.
