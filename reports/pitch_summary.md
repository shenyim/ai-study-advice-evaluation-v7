# Pitch Summary

## Project Name

AI Study Advice Evaluation Platform

## Positioning

A human-centered AI evaluation platform for comparing and improving AI-generated academic and career advice.

## Best Portfolio Sentence

I built a risk-aware AI evaluation platform that compares student-advice models, flags unsafe or low-quality answers, and turns failure patterns into product guardrails.

## Why It Matters

Students often ask AI systems questions that affect real decisions: course planning, graduate school preparation, research direction, coding skills, and career strategy. Average model quality is not enough. High-risk questions require stronger caution, verification, and human review.

## Demo Flow

1. Show the dashboard overview.
2. Open model comparison and explain risk-weighted scoring.
3. Show the review queue.
4. Open one high-risk case in the case review console.
5. Explain how the failure taxonomy leads to product fixes.
6. End with the roadmap: real model outputs, human annotation, inter-rater agreement, source verification.

## Interview Explanation

This project sits between data science, HCI, and responsible AI. The technical part is the evaluation pipeline and dashboard. The human-centered part is the rubric, risk segmentation, review workflow, and product guardrails.
